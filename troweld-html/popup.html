Plan and construct a new parking structure with ample EV charging stations to meet the growing demand for electric vehicles.

Variation 1

Multi-Level Parking Structure with Green Roofs

Project Name:

Urban Multi-Level Parking Garage

Location:

Main Street & Oak Avenue

Summary:

This variation involves constructing a multi-level parking garage that maximizes vertical space, allowing for more parking capacity in a smaller footprint. The garage will include green roofs for stormwater management and aesthetic purposes, and each floor will have LED lighting and electric vehicle charging stations. The design features a sleek, modern exterior with glass railings and landscaping around the base, contributing to a visually appealing structure that fits into the downtown vibe.

Key Features:

Three Levels of Parking: More space with vertical stacking Green Roof: Sustainable design with plants to reduce heat and manage rainwater Electric Vehicle (EV) Stations: 10% of spaces dedicated to EV charging LED Lighting: Energy-efficient and safe environment Modern Architecture: Glass railings, metal panels, and urban landscaping

Cost:

Estimated Cost: $14 million Maintenance Cost (Annual): $500,000

Benefits:

Maximizes space while maintaining the urban aesthetic Sustainable features like a green roof and EV stations Modern design that blends with the city's downtown

Parking Lot Details

Variation 2

Surface Lot with Modular, Sustainable Features

Project Name:

Sustainable Modular Parking Lot

Location:

Main Street & Oak Avenue

Summary:

In this variation, the existing surface parking lot will be reconfigured using modular, sustainable components. The lot will feature permeable pavement to reduce runoff, and solar panel-covered canopies to provide shade while generating clean energy. This design is less dense than the multi-level structure but focuses on making the lot more eco-friendly, efficient, and visually appealing. It will also include bike racks and EV charging spots, promoting eco-friendly transportation.

Key Features:

Permeable Pavement: Allows water to flow through, reducing flood risks Solar Panel Canopies: Energy generation while providing shade to cars Modular Design: Flexible layout that can be adjusted as demand changes Bike Racks: Encourages alternative transportation options EV Charging: Designated spots for electric vehicle charging

Cost:

Estimated Cost: $8 million Maintenance Cost (Annual): $300,000

Benefits:

Lower environmental impact with permeable paving and solar panels Flexible design that can be adjusted based on usage Less visual impact on the surrounding area than a multi-level structure Affordable, sustainable solution for parking needs
